🌸 About My Planner

A cozy productivity space made with love

✨ What is this?

My Planner is a cute productivity web app designed to help you organise tasks, stay motivated, and enjoy planning.

🎀 Why pixel-themed?

Planning shouldn’t feel stressful. The pixel style makes the experience fun, soft, and approachable.

☕ Who made this?

This project was created as a personal learning project, built to explore web development while creating something enjoyable to use.
